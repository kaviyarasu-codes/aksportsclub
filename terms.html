Booking & Payment

While booking, 50% advance payment is mandatory. The remaining amount must be paid before or at the time of play. Booking will be confirmed only after 50% advance payment.

100% refund will be provided if the booking is cancelled at least 3 hours before the scheduled time. No refund will be issued for cancellations made within 3 hours of the booking time.

Bookings can be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance, subject to availability. Rescheduling within 24 hours may incur additional charges.
